Responsible AI for Financial Operations

We are committed to the safe and responsible use of AI tools to enhance capabilities, automate routine tasks, and enable data-driven financial insights. As we streamline month-end reporting and financial analysis, data privacy and system security remain our top priority.

Governance & Procedures

Essential rules for the Finance team regarding data handling, tool requests, and incident reporting. Select a tab to review procedures.

Core Rules for Finance Data

  • ✓
    Comply with Data Protection You must not use AI tools in ways that violate Ravensdown policies or data protection obligations. Financial records, PII, and payroll data require strict handling.
  • ✗
    No Unauthorized External Sharing Do not upload sensitive financial data, internal reports, or source code with secrets into unapproved or public-facing AI models (e.g., standard free ChatGPT, Claude Web).
  • ℹ
    Approved Devices Only Use AI tools only on Ravensdown-issued devices (laptops, PCs, mobile phones). Consult IT for guidance on personal devices.

Reporting Data Incidents

If you suspect that sensitive financial or confidential information has been disclosed or compromised via an AI tool, act immediately.

👤

Step 1: Inform Manager

Immediately notify your direct manager or the Finance Director about the potential breach.

💻

Step 2: Contact IT

Submit a high-priority report to the IT department via RavHelp. The sooner you report, the faster impact is minimized.
